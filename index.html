About Us


A true family business, our food store has been dedicated to bringing the finest flavors from Europe and the best local food products to our community for over 20 years. We are proud to carry on a tradition of quality, crafting and selecting every item with care. Our large assortment of smoked goods is a testament to our craftsmanship, featuring a wide variety of expertly smoked sausages and hams. Our celebrated baked sausage, in particular, embodies the quality our customers have come to expect, cherished for its rich taste and superior preparation.

Find us nestled within the historic St. Lawrence Market, one of the area's most significant and renowned landmarks. We are honored to be a part of this vibrant community, and we invite you to stop by and experience the passion and exceptional flavors that our family has been sharing for two decades.
